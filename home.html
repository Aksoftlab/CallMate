Detailed Features

Caller ID & Call Screen

Instantly see who is calling with name, location (if available) and spam score. Customize the in-call screen and quick actions (message, record, block).

Call Recording

Record all calls automatically or use manual record. Save recordings in MP3 format with options to trim and share. Recordings are stored locally and can be backed up.

Spam Protection

Community-powered spam list and offline heuristics reduce unwanted calls. Report new spammers with a single tap.

Contact Management

Fast contact merge, custom tags and group messaging make large-address-book management painless. Search by name, number or tag.

FAQ

Is CallMate free?
Yes — core features are free. Some optional premium features (cloud backup, advanced spam lists) may require in-app purchase.

Do you store my call recordings?
No — recordings are saved on your device by default. You can export them manually or enable optional encrypted cloud backup.

Which Android versions are supported?
Android 7.0 (Nougat) and above.

Permissions

CallMate requests only the permissions necessary to deliver features: Phone (to identify incoming calls), Microphone (to record calls), Storage (to save recordings & backups), Contacts (to show caller names). The app will prompt permission requests at runtime — you can choose to grant or deny any permission.
