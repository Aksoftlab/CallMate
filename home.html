App Features (English)

  1. 1 — Complete loan record for every customer Maintain a full loan history per customer: outstanding amount, EMI schedule, last payments, next due date and custom notes.
  2. 2 — Google Sheets data management Connect your Google Sheets to manage and update master data — add, edit, and sync customer records directly from sheets.
  3. 3 — Fetched Google Sheets data stored in local storage Data fetched from Sheets is cached locally for fast access and offline usage — work in the field without constant connectivity.
  4. 4 — Direct Call, SMS & WhatsApp One-tap actions to call, send SMS, or open WhatsApp chat with the customer — saves agent time and improves contact rates.
  5. 5 — PTP (Promise To Pay) tracking & daily PTP tab Record PTP dates for customers. The PTP tab automatically shows today's PTPs so agents can prioritize follow-ups.
  6. 6 — Scheduled auto-calls Schedule automated calls at predefined times — the app places calls automatically to remind customers about payments.
  7. 7 — Backup & Restore to Google Drive Keep backups on Google Drive and restore data when needed — secure your customer databases and settings.
  8. 8 — Sequential calling list with feedback logging Call lists play one-by-one with a single click. After each call, agents can record feedback (conversation notes, PTP updates, add to PTP calendar).
  9. 9 — Notes & cash account management Maintain payment ledgers — log cash receipts, online payments, transfers and track which account received money.
  10. 10 — Visit planning & customer tagging Plan field visits, and tag customers as Favourite or Risky to prioritize actions and manage routes.
  11. 11 — Shows resolved & pending cases Quick view of resolved vs pending accounts so managers and agents instantly see work status.
  12. 12 — Daily performance tracking Track targets and achievement (%) per agent — see how much of the daily goal is complete and what remains.

How it helps collection teams

CallMate reduces manual work, increases contact success, and gives managers real-time visibility. Sync data from Google Sheets, set automated reminders, collect feedback on each call, and maintain auditable payment records.

Quick setup notes

  • Sign in with a Google account to enable Sheets and Drive integration.
  • Grant calling and SMS permissions on Android for one-tap dialing and messaging.
  • Use the PTP calendar to generate daily follow-up lists automatically.
